About Us

Canara Group of Companies is an esteemed supplier of sea food products. The legacy of excellence established by B.Mohammed has been carried forward by Mr. Hasan Nishal, who has brought his own unique vision and expertise to the company. Canara Group of Companies is a leading supplier of frozen sea food products, fruits, and vegetables. We have state-of-the-art facilities for processing and handling our products, including de-heading and mincing units. Our products are utilized in domestic animal diets, organic fertilizer for aquaculture, livestock feed, and other agricultural products. We are focused in our efforts to spread the word about the idea behind our products by our team of experts that are highly skilled in their respective fields. The team maintains a laser-like focus on the aim of maximizing productivity through ongoing initiatives to scale up the quality, output, and product advancements in a sustainable environment. Our team's versatility is one of our fundamental strengths. At the Mangalore port, our company employs 150 to 200 personnel who are responsible for purchasing raw materials, mincing, de-heading, packaging, and transportation.
