About us

Canara Marine Fisheries has a rich history dating back to 1983 when it was founded with the primary aim of sourcing raw materials from the port and supplying them to other processing units. As the business evolved, we gained a deep understanding of every element of the industry and began supplying high-grade, export-quality fish to various exporters. Our continued development and growth have been instrumental in contributing to the success of the business.

Products

Our company supplies raw materials that are essential forthe production of fish meal, fish oil, and fish soluble. These valuable materials have multiple applications and are highly sought afterin various industries, including animal feed, agriculture, and health supplements. Our products are of high-quality, ensuring that the end products meet the needs and expectations of our clients.
