About us

Canara Marine Products, underthe guidance of Mr. Hassan Nishal's visionary leadership, has been able to expand its operations and venture into new areas.In 2008, the company introduced Canara Marine Products, which allowed it to procure a wide range of raw materials to meet the needs of various industries and clients. With its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, the company quickly established a strong foothold in the market. By 2010, Canara Marine Fisheries had further expanded its sourcing operations to other states such as Maharashtra, Goa, Kerala, and Tamil Nadu. This enabled the company to meet the diverse requirements ofits clients and supply raw materials for a broad spectrum of products.
